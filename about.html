Established in 2020, Glee is an Incense company, guided by the passion for fragrances. Made from purest oils and ingredients, we have the best range of premium incense sticks including - Flora Muse, Musk Love, White Sandal and Secret Crush.
Glee is exploring new ways of generating high-quality scent while holding low smoke emissions. Glee incense sticks are less smoke and high in fragrance, using the new techniques and high-quality raw materials.While we emphasize high-quality scent with low smoke emissions, we also want to contribute to society.
Glee intends to have a social impact by spreading fragrances.Glee has made a mission to contribute to the education sector by providing underprivileged kids with books and other educational stuff.
