Established in 2020, Glee is an incense sticks company, guided by the passion for fragrances. Made from purest oils and ingredients, we have the best range of premium incense sticks including - Flora Muse, Musk Love, White Sandal and Secret Crush.

Glee is exploring new ways of generating high-quality scent while holding low smoke emissions. Using new techniques and high-quality raw materials glee has achieved the goal and provides high quality incense sticks generating less smoke. While we emphasize high-quality scent with low smoke emissions, we also want to contribute to society.
